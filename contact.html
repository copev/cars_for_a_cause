Do you have any questions, or want to get involved?

Fill out this form if you have any questions for our team, want to get involved, or simply want to talk. We are here to help all members of the Windsor-Essex community and can't wait to put on a great show!

We've also included a list of resources for anyone who feels they might benefit from seeking help. We believe in embracing the idea that it's ok not to be okay. If you ever feel at risk to yourself, or know of someone who is in need of help, please take the time explore our resources and other options.
