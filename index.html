the charities we're supporting

Since 2019, Cars for a Cause has donated over $30,000 to local organizations supporting mental health. This year, we have selected Noah's House and Youth Wellness Hub Windsor-Essex as our charity beneficiaries. Our goal for 2022 is to raise $15,000 for local youth mental health initiatives.

About Youth Wellness Hub

The Youth Wellness Hub Windsor-Essex offers free services to youth ages 12 to 25 without a wait list or referral. This one-stop-shop model of care allows for a multitude of services all under one roof in a youth friendly space.

See the Wellness Hub

About Noah's House

Noah's House is a mental health charity in Windsor, Ontario. Noah's House was established in 2018 after Noah Butcher-Hagell lost his battle with mental illness. Noah's House is working to fill the gap on mental health support in a non-clinical setting for individuals ages 11-25.
